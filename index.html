Wouldn't it be nice if we had a context for every news story? Who wants us to know this information? Who has something to gain from us believing this story? It has been estimated that over 75% of the news is propaganda. Machine learning techniques can help distinguish between what is news and what is not in the same way that a spam filter can accurately determine which emails are spam and which emails are authentic. As these algorithms learn, they will be able to determine not only what is spin but also why it is being spun. We are hiring machine learning specialists and data scientists that want to push the news to become a better product, that can help separate the wheat from the chaff.

News Detective filters the content of the news and many pieces of metadata associated with each story in order to predict which stories are authentic and which stories are published because some powerful group wants to shape your opinions. News Detective includes a couple of sentences at the beginning of each news story to help you to read the news with a different frame of reference. Does this article benefit a single company, an industry, a lobbying group, a political party? Is there important legislation under consideration? Is this story a distraction away from a more important story? News Detective is working to answer all of these questions and more. What's more, in answering these questions, you will have a significant amount of news without agenda to read and will have learned what the agendas are with the remaining stories.
